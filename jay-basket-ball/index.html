JAY BASKETBALL

Shoot the purple J basketball into the hoop. Score 20 points for every basket and try to achieve the highest score possible before running out of lives.

HOW TO PLAY

1. Drag the basketball to aim.
2. Release to shoot.
3. Get the ball into the hoop.
4. Every basket gives +10 points.
5. Missing costs 1 life.
6. Lose all lives and game over.
